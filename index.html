About University

JSS SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY UNIVERSITY

JSS STU is the second University established by JSS Mahavidyapeetha, besides the JSS Academy of Higher Education and Research a health sciences focused deemed university at Mysuru.

JSS STU plays a key role in providing high quality skilled manpower in the fields of Science, Engineering & Technology and Management to transform the society through research and innovation.

JSS STU is committed to deliver quality education at all levels and provide opportunities for aspiring youth to meet the global needs of society in every sector.

SRI JAYACHAMARAJENDRA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Established in 1963 as SJCE has 12 departments in engineering, a Master of Computer Applications department. It was affiliated to the VTU, Belgaum, but now it's a Constituent College of JSS Science and Technology University from 2016 - 2017 academic year. SJCE is accredited by the AICTE.

Research and Innovation

Academic Research

JSS STU, rooted in the esteemed legacy of SJCE, Mysuru, is fervently committed to advancing Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Management through cutting-edge research and innovation. Recognized for its dedication to tackling contemporary challenges, the university is a hub for impactful academic research and technological advancements. Faculty members spearhead pioneering research endeavours, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in their respective fields. Concurrently, graduate students actively contribute to research through coursework and dissertations, while undergraduates, whether in their coursework or engaged in internships and research fellowships, are encouraged to participate in the institution's culture of innovation. Renowned for its academic excellence in professional programs and a holistic approach, JSS STU University stands out as a premier destination for those seeking to make significant contributions to the realms of research and innovation in Science and Engineering.

Research and Laboratory

At JSS STU University, each department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, exemplifying the institution's commitment to providing a conducive environment for cutting-edge research and innovation. These laboratories are furnished with the necessary equipment and resources, ensuring that students and faculty have access to the tools required for their academic and research pursuits.
